A Word About Open-source

All the software in this book and the libraries it uses - and WebGL itself - are open-source, i.e. you are free to use and modify them with the restriction that you retain any credits and license that is in the software source.

Open-source is a wonderful boon to the web as it allows people from all over to work together and collaborate free of charge and without legal restrictions (save the caveat listed above). Please respect the efforts of all the people who have worked hard and given back by respecting the very limited requirements of the licenses.
